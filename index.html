The Problem

You've Tried Tricks.
They Didn't Work. Here's Why.

"Guess the word from the picture." "Sound it out — sort of." "It'll click eventually."

These aren't systems. They're guesses dressed up as methods — and millions of people are paying the price. Functional illiteracy is not a failure of intelligence. It is the silent consequence of being handed a map with half the roads missing.

Reading is a code. Like any code, it can be broken down, taught step by step, and mastered by anyone — but only if someone teaches the actual code, not a shortcut around it.

Silent exclusion happens one unread form, one skipped opportunity, one misunderstood message at a time. The Reading Blueprint exists to end that.

The Programme

Four Phases. One Complete Journey.

Every skill builds on the last. Nothing is introduced before the learner is ready.

Sound Foundations

Alphabet, individual sounds, vowels, and fundamental patterns — no gaps, no assumptions.

Phase 02

Consonant blends, digraphs, and the six syllable types that govern English word structure.

Phase 03

Architecture of Meaning

Sentence structure, punctuation, and the foundations of reading with real purpose.

Phase 04

Achieving Fluency

Speed, expression, phrasing, and deep comprehension — the marks of a confident reader.

The Method

Why This Works When Others Don't

⟶

Controlled Blending

Every word decoded one sound at a time — left to right, no guessing. Builds the neural pathway that makes fluent reading automatic.

◉

The Chin Test

A physical technique for counting syllables. Your own body becomes a reliable learning tool — no abstract rules to memorise.

✓

Controlled Texts

Practice passages use only words containing sounds already taught. Every exercise is designed to be completed successfully — building confidence alongside competence.
