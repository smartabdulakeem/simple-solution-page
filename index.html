The Problem

You've Tried Tricks.
They Didn't Work. Here's Why.

"Guess the word from the picture." "Sound it out — sort of." "It'll click eventually."

These aren't systems. They're guesses dressed up as methods — and millions of people are paying the price. Functional illiteracy is not a failure of intelligence. It is the silent consequence of being handed a map with half the roads missing.

The crisis of adults and children who cannot read fluently is not a mystery. Reading is a code. Like any code, it can be broken down, taught step by step, and mastered by anyone — but only if someone teaches the actual code, not a shortcut around it.

Silent exclusion happens one unread form, one skipped opportunity, one misunderstood message at a time. The Reading Blueprint exists to end that.

The Method

The Special Sauce:
Why This Works When Others Don't

Three principles separate The Reading Blueprint from every other reading programme you've tried.

⟶

Controlled Blending

Every word is decoded one sound at a time — left to right, no guessing. Controlled blending trains the brain to see a word as a sequence of sounds, building the neural pathway that makes fluent reading automatic.

◉

The Chin Test

A simple, physical technique for counting syllables — your chin drops once for every syllable you speak. No abstract rules to memorise. Just your own body as a reliable learning tool.

✓

Controlled Texts

Practice passages use only words that contain sounds already taught. This one rule eliminates failure. Every reading exercise is designed to be completed successfully — building confidence alongside competence.
